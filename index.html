Stocks

We invest in hot stocks locally as well as outside the borders. These are mainly companies that are undervalued and have the potential to grow exponentially in the near future. Bonds

We buy bonds sold by the government seeing the interest rates of developing countries like Kenya is relatively high as compared to developed ones.
